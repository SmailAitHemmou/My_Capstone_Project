About the Project

WatchLog is a personal web app designed to help users track their favorite movies and series. It allows you to manage your watchlist, explore new content, and stay organized with an elegant interface. It also maintains the privacy of each user, as only you can see and control your viewing list.

Our Mission

At WatchLog, our mission is to enhance your entertainment experience by providing a seamless and user-friendly platform for tracking your viewing habits. We believe that keeping a log of your watched content can enrich your appreciation for movies and series while helping you stay organized.
